D2L Dark Mode

Dark Mode Apply dark theme to all D2L pages

Inverts page colors. Images and videos are preserved automatically.

Document Dark Mode Darken documents (PDFs, slides, etc.) in the viewer

Inverts document text and backgrounds. Diagrams may look different.

Video Dark Mode Darken embedded videos (Echo360, YouTube, etc.)

Off by default. Videos play in original colors unless this is enabled.
